__Problem: Write a program to calculate the sum of even numbers in a list of integers. 
__Input_Desc: The input data is a sequence of numbers, the first number is the number of elements in the list, the remaining numbers are the numbers in the list.
__Output_Desc: The output is a single integer representing the sum of all even numbers in the list.
__Test_Case:
3 3 2 1 -> 2
5 3 5 7 9 1 -> 0
4 3 2 4 6 -> 12
